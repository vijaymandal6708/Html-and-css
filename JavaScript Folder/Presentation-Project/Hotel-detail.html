About this property


Comfortable Accommodations: Super Townhouse Nagpur Railway Station in Nagpur offers family rooms with air-conditioning, private bathrooms, work desks, and seating areas. Each room includes free toiletries, bath or shower, TV, tiled floors, and a wardrobe.

Essential Facilities: Guests enjoy free WiFi, a lift, 24-hour front desk, housekeeping service, room service, and luggage storage. The hotel is pet-friendly, ensuring a pleasant stay for all travellers.

Dining Options: A continental breakfast is available each morning. Additional amenities include a TV, work desk, and free toiletries, catering to diverse guest needs.

Prime Location: Located 9 km from Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar International Airport, the hotel is a 5-minute walk from Nagpur Railway Station. Nearby attractions include Sitabuldi Fort and Zero Mile Stone Nagpur, each 2 km away.
